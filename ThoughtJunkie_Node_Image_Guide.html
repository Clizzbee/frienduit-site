🎨 ThoughtJunkie Visual Aesthetic

Images that feel native to the dark, glowing, sci-fi mind-map environment look best in-app.

App Color Palette (for tonal matching)

✅ Styles That Work Great

Glowing neon on dark, cinematic concept art, cosmic/space scenes, cel-shaded icons, bokeh light effects, watercolor on dark paper, synthwave aesthetics, holographic / iridescent, minimalist icons with transparency, flat-design illustrations.

⚠️ Styles to Use Carefully

Bright white / stark white backgrounds (will conflict with dark canvas), photorealistic photos with cluttered backgrounds (details lost at small node sizes), light pastel palettes (can look washed out against dark backgrounds), dense text or small labels in the image.

✅ Checklist Before Prompting

  • 📐Square format specified? Always say "square", "1:1", or "1024x1024" (or both). Never let the AI pick an aspect ratio.
  • 🎯Subject centered? The app uses cover-fill scaling. Off-center subjects get cropped out.
  • 🔵Shape composition modifier added? Circle/diamond/hexagon/heart each need their own composition hint.
  • 🌑Dark background specified? Say "dark navy background", "#0f172a background", or "deep black background".
  • 🚫No text / no UI / no borders specified? Prevents the AI from adding unreadable labels or frames.
  • 🖼️Resolution at 512+ ? At minimum 512 × 512. 1024 × 1024 is ideal for large nodes.
  • 💾Saving as PNG? Preserves quality, supports transparency. Prefer over JPEG for icons/illustrations.
  • 🎨Color matches node intent? Optionally match image tones to the node's glow color for cohesive look.

⚡ Do's and Don'ts

✅ DO

  • Center your main subject in all cases
  • Use dark or transparent backgrounds
  • Generate square images (1:1)
  • Include "no text, no watermarks, no borders"
  • Match the image's glow color to node color
  • Use high contrast between subject and background
  • Save as PNG for illustrations/icons
  • Think about what the image looks like at tiny sizes (60px node)
  • Add soft vignette or feathered edges for shapes that clip

❌ DON'T

  • Use white/bright backgrounds — clashes with dark canvas
  • Put important content in corners — gets clipped on circles/hearts/diamonds
  • Include tiny text — illegible at small node sizes
  • Use portrait/landscape aspect ratios — will be cropped asymmetrically
  • Generate low resolution (under 256px)
  • Use complex scenes with many competing focal points
  • Rely on the bottom-third for heart shapes
  • Forget that nodes can glow — high-saturation images can clash with intense glow effects
